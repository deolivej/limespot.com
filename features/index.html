Features

A Personalized Catalog for Each of your Shoppers


  • Present personalized product offerings based on your shoppers' individual tastes and interests
  • Fine-tune your store to change its appearance and media content in response to a customer's demographic
  • Easy Gifting: customers can find products that match their friends' interests in two clicks

  • Unlimited depth of personalization based on the targeting information you provide about your products.
  • Your personalized store can be put on your Facebook page and your website

Promote Your Brand


  • Make posts that only show up to selected target audiences in just two clicks.
  • Engage your shopper by providing social shopping features
  • Increase your business exposure, grow your audience and ultimately increase your cross-sales with your Personalized Store content.

Real-Time Analytical Reports


  • Get deep social insights about the demographics of your audience
  • Discover the products that engage your customers
  • Take recommended actions based on your shoppers' behaviour

Simple to Use


  • Enter your business information
  • Select your target audiences
  • Choose a catalog template

  • No technical knowledge required: anyone can create and manage a LimeSpot-powered store

Integrate with E-Commerce & Payment Systems Seamlessly


  • Integrate with your existing e-commerce system within two clicks
  • Integrate with major payment providers like PayPal, Amazon, and Facebook
  • Update your product portfolio on your regular e-commerce platform and your personalized LimeSpot store will synchronize with the changes in seconds

  • No need to learn a new interface
  • No need to set up or manage a new inventory, tax/shipment or payment channel
  • No need to update your LimeSpot store after setup â€“ it will sync automatically

Customize your Personalized Store


  • Choose from different Catalog Templates
  • Choose from different Color Themes
  • Customize your store's look & feel by adding your logo and editing text, menus, and graphics
  • Graphic UI can generate new, tailor-made templates for your storefront

  • No technical knowledge needed for customization

Let Your Shoppers Socialize


Shoppers can:

  • Share and tweet your products
  • Add your items to their wishlists and check their friends' wishlists to find gifts
  • Make group purchases
  • Interact with your brand and ask for special prices or packaging
  • Chat about particular products with each other

  • Online shopping becomes a fun social activity like it is in real life

Cloud Computing & Privacy Control


  • Based on Microsoft Azure cloud computing platform
  • SSL encryption
  • 99.99% uptime
  • No private information will be saved or shared with any third party
  • No hosting or maintenance
