Shipping and Tax

Shipping and Tax information should be set up for your PayPal merchant account in order to sell products with LimeSpot. For steps on how to do this, see below.

  1. Log in to your PayPal account.
  2. Go to My Account, then Profile.
  3. Go to My Selling Tools.
  4. Select Update for Sales Tax and set your tax calculations for different countries and/or states.
  5. Select Update for Shipping and set your shipping rates.
