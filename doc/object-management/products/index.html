Products

Products are individual items that you wish to sell on your LimeSpot powered online store. Products are often physical goods, but they can also be digital files like eBooks or music; or services like rentals and extended warranties.

Products can be added to your store from multiple sources depending on your business requirements. Refer to Import and Set Up Products for details.

LimeSpot keeps track of all your products and related product attributes on the Products page.

You can perform the following tasks from the Products page:

  • Add a New Product: Click on the Add Product buton in the Top Right corner of the page.
  • View product as shown on your online shop: Click the Action button and select Runtime.
  • Edit product details: Click on the Action button and select Edit.
  • Create a copy of Product: Click on the Action button and select Copy.
  • Delete a Product: Click on the Action button and select Delete.

Similar products are grouped into Collections which are then inserted into Catalogs to generate customizable online shops. Refer to Add Products to Catalog for details.

Targeting Rules and Keywords can be applied to Products so as to display personalized product catalogs based on shopper's social profile. Refer to Targeting Rules for details.
