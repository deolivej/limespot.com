Products

Products are individual items that you wish to sell on your LimeSpot powered online store. Products are often physical goods, but they can also be digital files like eBooks, music or services like rentals and extended warranties.

LimeSpot keeps track of all your products and related product attributes on Product page.

Products can be added to your store from multiple sources depending upon the nature of your business. Refer to Import and Setup Products and Collections for details.

You can see the list of all your Products and related attributes by visiting the Product section of Administration panel.

You can perform the following tasks from Products page:

  • Add a New Product: Click on the + icon on Top left.
  • Add Multiple Products by importing Excel file: Click on the (image here) icon
  • View product as seen on your online Shop: Click on the (image here) icon.
  • Create a copy of Product: Click on the Copy icon
  • Edit product details: Click on Edit icon.
  • Delete a Product: Click on the Delete icon

Products are added to Catalogs to create customizable online storefront.

You can add multiple Products and Collections to a single product catalog based on your business needs. Refer to section Add Products to Catalog for details.

Targeting Rules can be applied to Products so as to create personalize product catalogs for different shoppers.
