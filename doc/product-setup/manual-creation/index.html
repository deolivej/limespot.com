Manual Creation of Products

Small and medium-sized businesses with a limited number of products can use this feature to manually create products one at a time from the Admin Panel.

The Products section of the Admin Panel is where you can manipulate and view your products. Products will be listed here no matter how they were uploaded to LimeSpot.

The landing page of the Products section looks like this:

From this page you can:

  • Manually create new products.
  • Import multiple products using an Excel spreadsheet.
  • View products as they are seen to customers.
  • Create copies of products.
  • Edit product details.
  • Delete products.

How to Manually Create New Products

  1. Select the Products tab from the Admin Panel.
  2. Click on the + icon present at the top left corner of the page. You will see the following screen.
  3. Enter a Title for your product.
  4. Select a Product Type from the Drop down list.
  5. Enter a Product Description that will describe the product to shoppers.
  6. Verify that the status of the Active checkbox is as you want it. The Active checkbox makes the product visible or invisible to your customers.
  7. Upload images of your product for customers to view. You can upload a maximum of 5 images from either your local machine or a website URL that contains an image file.
  8. Add Product Variants representing different versions of the product (more details below).
  9. Choose appropriate Targeting Rules* that you wish to associate with the Product. These will define which customers your products appear to.
  10. Click on the Save button to complete the Process.

* Our system provides a pre-generated list of Targeting Rules that cover most widely used conditions. However, you are free to create your own Targeting Rules from the Admin Panel.

Product Variants

Product Variants are used to record different versions (such as different styles, colors, sizes, etc.) of a single product. For example, variants on a jacket could include small, medium, and large versions. LimeSpot gives you the freedom to choose multiple Product Attributes for different product variants.

Product attributes that should be entered for every new product variant are as follows.

  1. Title: the title of your product variant, related to size, color etc.
  2. Barcode: if applicable, the barcode number can be used for inventory tracking and billing.
  3. SKU: the SKU code helps identify products variants.
  4. Price: the price (excluding taxes) that you want to set for that product variant.
  5. Original Price: if a product has been discounted, you can show its original price here so customers will see how much they are saving.
  6. Stock Override: this checkbox enables your store to continue selling a product variant even after its Stock Quantity has reached zero.
  7. Stock Quantity: this field indicates the available stock quantity of a particular product variant.
  8. Active: this checkbox is used to make a products variant visible or invisible in your LimeSpot store.

In addition to the fields mentioned above, you can choose up to a maximum of 5 custom product attributes that suit your industry needs. Refer to Store Setup for details.

Every new product should be associated with an appropriate Product Type that groups related products under a single identity. This is useful feature for showcasing all products of a specific category in your store instead of individual products. Refer to Creating Product Types for details.
